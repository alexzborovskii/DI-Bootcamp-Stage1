Animal shelter in the Leningrad region, Russia

Karat was found near the fence of the shelter, tied to a tree. His neck was injured by his strict collar. He was scared. Few employees were bitten while trying to help him.
Finaly Karat was taken inside the shelter and received medical treatment.
His ears were sad. Few months later shelter began to look for his forever-family.

Saint-Petersburg, Russia

Forever-family. First step to success

It was love at a first sight. I took him home emmediately.
He never bit me, but always had behavioral problems whith other animals. He learnd to cope with it at special courses.
Karat was with us everywhere he could - explored the nature and guarded his family.

Israel

Final success of an orphan from shelter.

Some people offered us to leave the dog in Russia, becaouse it's too difficult and expensive to move somevere whit a dog. Flight also was not easy for such an amotional dog. But it was all worth it.
Karat enjoys the sand of the Israelian beaches, jumps into the sea and frightened runs out.
Sometimes Kara even tryes to start new friendship (but still barks and tryes to atack after few seconds. We're working on it.)
